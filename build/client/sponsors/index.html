Sponsors

First off, thanks to all who sponsor  our parent, the Apache Software Foundation.

The below companies have been gracious enough to provide their commercial tool offerings free of charge to the Apache HBase™ project.

  • The crew at ej-technologies  have been let us use JProfiler  for years now.
  • The lads at headway software  have given us a license for Restructure101  so we can untangle our interdependency mess.
  • YourKit  allows us to use their Java Profiler .
  • Some of us use IntelliJ IDEA  thanks to JetBrains .
  • Thank you to Boris at Vector Portal  for granting us a license on the image  on which our logo is based.

Sponsoring the Apache Software Foundation

To contribute to the Apache Software Foundation, a good idea in our opinion, see the ASF Sponsorship  page.
